Here are our top 3 child-friendly routes around Sheffield!

Damflask Reservoir


  • 5.60km
  • 1hr 40m


This route is an easy going, circular route around the reservoir.
It follows well surfaced pathways, with short sections of pavements and roads. The route is mostly flat with gentle slopes.
Perfect for prams, bikes & little feet!

Difficulty Rating:

Broomhead Reservoir


  • 4.50km
  • 1hr 15m


Located on Sheffield's doorstep, Moor Hall circular is a peaceful walk in the Peak District National Park, taking in woodlands and glimpses across the reservoir.
This is a very popular area for birding, hiking, and running, so you'll likely encounter other people while exploring.
Dogs are welcome and may be off-lead in some areas.

Difficulty Rating:

Redmires Reservoir


  • 4.20km
  • 1hr


A very popular route for walking.
This peaceful circular walk crosses three reservoirs.
As you descend you will witness plenty of wildlife who love to visit the reservoir too!

Difficulty Rating:
